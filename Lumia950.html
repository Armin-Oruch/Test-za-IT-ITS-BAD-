Details

Microsoft Lumia 950 mobile was launched in October 2015. The phone comes with a 5.20-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1440x2560 pixels at a pixel density of 564 pixels per inch (ppi). Microsoft Lumia 950 is powered by a 1.8GHz hexa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 808 processor. It comes with 3GB of RAM. The Microsoft Lumia 950 runs Windows 10 Mobile and is powered by a 3000mAh removable battery.
As far as the cameras are concerned, the Microsoft Lumia 950 on the rear packs 20-megapixel camera. It sports a 5-megapixel camera on the front for selfies.
Microsoft Lumia 950 is based on Windows 10 Mobile and packs 32GB of inbuilt storage that can be expanded via microSD card (up to 200GB). The Microsoft Lumia 950 is a single SIM (GSM) mobile that accepts a Nano-SIM card. The Microsoft Lumia 950 measures 145.00 x 73.20 x 8.20mm (height x width x thickness) and weighs 150.00 grams.
Connectivity options on the Microsoft Lumia 950 include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, GPS, Bluetooth v4.10, NFC, FM radio, 3G, and 4G (with support for Band 40 used by some LTE networks in India). Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, barometer, gyroscope, proximity sensor, and compass/ magnetometer.
