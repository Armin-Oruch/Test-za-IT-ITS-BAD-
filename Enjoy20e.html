Details

The Huawei Enjoy 20e (2022) runs HarmonyOS 2 and is powered by a 5000mAh battery.
As far as the cameras are concerned, the Huawei Enjoy 20e (2022) on the rear packs a dual camera setup featuring a 13-megapixel primary camera with an f/1.8 aperture, and a 2-megapixel camera with an f/2.4 aperture. The rear camera setup has autofocus. It has a single front camera setup for selfies, featuring an 8-megapixel sensor with an f/2.0 aperture. The front camera also features autofocus.
Huawei Enjoy 20e (2022) is based on HarmonyOS 2 and packs 128GB of inbuilt storage that can be expanded via microSD card (up to 512GB) with a dedicated slot. The Huawei Enjoy 20e (2022) is a dual-SIM (GSM and GSM) mobile that accepts Nano-SIM and Nano-SIM cards. It was launched in Magic Night Black, Phantom Violet, and Qijing Forest colours.
Connectivity options on the Huawei Enjoy 20e (2022) include Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, GPS, Bluetooth v5.00, USB OTG, Micro-USB, 3G, and 4G (with support for Band 40 used by some LTE networks in India). Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, proximity sensor, and fingerprint sensor. The Huawei Enjoy 20e (2022) supports face unlock.
